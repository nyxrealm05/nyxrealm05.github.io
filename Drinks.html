TEAS

Thai Milk Tea (single serve)

Recipe
AmountIngredientsSteps
1/4 Cup Thai tea dry mix 1. Prepare a tall glass with a fine cloth mesh strainer and add thai tea mix
1 Cup Boiling Water 2. Pour boiling water over tea and use a spoon to press down on mix.
1 Cup Ice 3. Use your hands and squeeze the tea in the mesh bag (careful its hot!!). Remove bag when done.
2-3 Tbsp Sweetened Condensed Milk 4. Add ice. Add condensed milk and mix well.
1/2 - 1 Cup Whole milk or milk of Choice 5. Add your milk of choice, and boba pearls if you'd like, a straw and enjoy!!

One of my favorite milk teas, Thai Milk tea is such a classic it never gets old and having a glass of this is a sugar rush but much appreciated. I'll be trying this recipe again soon but going to make it a bit more sugar friendly since I heard theres even sugar free sweetened condensed milk out there.

Chai w/ Honey Cardamom Cold Foam

Recipe
AmountIngredientsSteps
1 Chai tea bag 1. Prepare a mug and fill with water and microwave 1 min + 30 secs.
8 oz Water 2. Add chai tea bag into hot water and let steep 5 minutes.
1/2 Tbsp Honey 3. In a seperate small bowl add heavy cream, sugar, and cardaomom and with a milk frother mix till soft peaks form. Set aside.
2 Tbsp Heavy Cream 4. Remove tea bag. Add honey and mix well.
1 tsp Sugar 5. Top tea with milk foam and sprinkle extra cardomom on top and enjoy!!
Pinch of Cardamom

Made this drink on a whim when using up the second to last of my tazo chai tea bags and it turned out sooo good that I immedietaly made it again the next day. The taste is just phenomenal and the cardamom in the foam just takes it to a whole new level. I will be making this again for sure!

Brown Sugar Vanilla Iced Coffee

Recipe

Ingredients

AmountItemSteps
2 Tbsp Brown Sugar 1. Prepare a glass and add brown sugar, coffee, and hot water.
2 Tbsp Instant Coffee 2. Using a milk frother mix until fluffy.
1/2 tsp Vanilla extract 3. Add enough ice to meet half of your cup.
2 Tbsp Hot water 4. Add your milk to your preference, stir with your straw and enjoy!!
Ice
Milk of choice

One of the first iced coffee recipes I tried out when I got really into making coffee drinks at home and its an easy and affordable way to make a cafe style drink, at home and since we alwyas have some Nescafe on hand its super easy.
