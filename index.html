Highlight of the Month
October 2025: Bulgogi Rice Noodle Bowls

Vietnamese inspired dish with Korean flavors since the pork belly marinade I tried for the first time was a Hit or miss and I did not want to risk the family not eating. I used a mix of rice noodles and sweet potato noodles, chopped up fresh veggies like green leaf lettuce and cilantro (my fav) and made a quick vietnamese pickled carrots. Protein was Bulgogi beef and I topped it all with some sweet chili sauce and a bit of laoganma. A hit all around and will be making again soon!
