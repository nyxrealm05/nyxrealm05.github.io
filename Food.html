Scallion Pancakes

Recipe
AmountIngredientsSteps
2 cups All purpose flour 1. In a large bowl, add flour, some salt, and water. Stir with two chopsticks til combined.
1 cup + 1 Tbsp warm water 2. Knead with your hands until dough is formed. Cover and let rest for 15 minutes.
3. While dough is resting, heat a pan with your oil. Chop your scallions and add only the whites into hot oil and stir until browned.
2 - 3 stalks of scallion 4. In a small ramaken, add your flour and salt. Once scallion oil is ready pour oil into flour mixture and mix well.
2 tsp salt 5. Roll dough into a log and cut it into 6 equal size pieces.
1 Tbsp All purpose flour 6. Take one dough ball and roll out into thin rectangle. Spoon in scallion oil mixture and sprinkle in chopped green scallion parts.
1/3 cup olive oil 7. Fold long sides of rectangle and meet at the center. Fold in half and stretch out dough a bit and slap on surface. Roll long dough sheet into a spiral. Repeat x 5.
8. Flatten spiral and roll out into flat pancake shape of your choosing. In a heated skillet add some oil and fry your pancakes, flipping every couple minutes until golden on both sides. Enjoy warm!

On one of my many online grocery shopping ventures on Weee app, I discovered these and purchased. Let me just say, I became enlightened and NEEDED to learn how to make them. I have made these atleast 3 times already and they are still as good as I remember. A bit of a labor od love with all the folds but they matter because they give that flaky interior that is integral to making this chinese street food. Theres Indian versions too I have found but ya know food is all connected. Carby but 100% worth it and its healthy cause it has greens :).

Easy no Knead Crusty Bread

Recipe
AmountIngredientsSteps
3 Cups Flour 1. In a large bowl, mix flour, yeast and salt. Add water, then use the handle of wooden spoon to mix untial all the flour is incorporated. Dough will be wet and sloppy-not kneadable. Adjust water and flour as needed.
2 tsp instant or rapid rise yeast 2. Rise: Cover with cling wrap or plate, leave on counter for 2 - 3 hours until it doubles in volume,
2 tsp cooking/kosher salt 3. Optional - refrigerate for flavour development (At this stage, you can either bake immediately or refrigerate for up to 3 days).
1 1/2 cups very warm tap water 4. Take chill out of refrigerated dough: if you refrigerated dough per above, leave the bowl on the counter for 45 - 60 minutes while the oven is preheating..
Extra flour dusting 5. Preheat oven: Put dutch oven in oven with lid on (26cm/10" or larger). Preheat to 450°F, 30 minutes prior to baking.
2 Tbsp Butter, melted 6. Shape dough: Sprinkle work surface with 1 tbsp flour, scrape dough out of bowl. Sprinkle top with 1/2 tbsp flour.
7. Using a dough scraper or anything of similar shape (cake server, large knife, spatula), fold the sides inwards (about 6 folds) to roughly form a roundish shape. Don't overdo it or it'll deflate.
8. ransfer to paper: Slide a large piece of parchment/baking paper (not wax paper) next to the dough, then flip the dough upside down onto the paper (ie seam side down, smooth side up). Slide/push it towards the middle, then reshape it into a round(ish) shape.
9. Dough in pot: Remove piping hot dutch oven from oven. Use paper to place dough into pot, place lid on.
10. Bake 30 minutes covered, then 12 minutes uncovered or until deep golden and crispy.
11. Cool on rack for 10 minutes before slicing.

My mom always asks me to make this bread we love fresh bread in our house, especially her, and this recipe never misses. It is one of the easiest and most low maintenance bread recipes I have ever tried and I really should make it a habit to have i ready in the fridge so I can have fresh bread on the same day when I want it. Sighhh anyways, super easy and super reliable bread recipe I have made atleast 4 times now and it has yet to fail me. Also, super delicious, especially with some kerrygold butter AHHH im drooling.

Easy No-Bake Tiramisu

Recipe
AmountIngredientsSteps
1 1/4 cup Heavy cream 1. Using either a stand mixer with a whip attachment, or a handheld electric mixer on high speed, whip the heavy cream to stiff peaks.
1/4 cup Granulated sugar 2. Reduce the mixer speed to medium, slowly add the granulated sugar and vanilla extract. Mix for 1 to 1 ½ minutes.
1 tsp Vanilla extract 3. Lower the mixer speed to low and add in the mascarpone cheese. Mix just until all the ingredients are incorporated. Set this aside.
8 oz Mascarpone cheese, room temperature 4. In a small shallow bowl, add the cold espresso, or strong cold coffee. If you are using the coffee flavored liqueur, stir it in with the espresso.
2/3 cup cold espresso, or cold strong coffee 5. Dip both sides, do not soak, each lady finger in the espresso mixture.
2 Tbsp kahlua (coffee flavored liquer) 6. Lay the dipped lady fingers in a single layer in the bottom of an 8 x 8 baking dish.
24 Lady fingers 7. Using a silicone spatula, evenly spread half of the cheese mixture on top of the lady fingers layer.
2 1/2 Tbsp Cocoa powder 8. Repeat the dipped lady fingers layer, and the remaining half cheese mixture layer.
9. Using a fine sifter, dust the top of the cheese mixture with the cocoa powder. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 3 hours to overnight.

If you can't tell by now, I am a big fan of easy recipes. That's why this recipe I made for my tiramisu-loving friend vivi is a fanstastic option. It comes together so easily and I was actually requested by my friends to bring it for our Halloween party this year so I will be tripling this so everyone can have some. I don't have much of a reference to authentic tiramisu but heck this is easier to make so I'll just stick with this.

Coffee Jelly

Recipe

Ingredients

AmountItemSteps
1 cup Boiling water 1. Add your intant coffee, gelatin powder, brown sugar, and boiling water into a large jug. Whisk until sugar has dissolved and ensure no gelatin stuck to bottom.
1 cup Cold water 2. Pour in cold water and whisk again.
1/3 cup Brown sugar 3. Pour in the coffee jelly into either dessert cups or glass dish..
3 tsp gelatin powder 4.*Optional: Using whisk, try to break any large bubbles around the edges for a nice smooth finish once set.
2 Tbsp Instant coffee 5. Place into fridge for atleast 3 hours to set (it will still be warm, but the cold water brings temperature down enough to pop straight into the fridge).
1 tsp sugar 6. If using a dish, cut jelly into cubes and scoop out into serving dishes.
1/4 cup Heavy Cream 7.When serving, add any garnishes you want. If cubed, whisk together heavy cream, sugar, and vanilla till combined and pour in. If in dessert cup, whisk mixture until soft peaks form and top. Enjoy!!
Splash of Vanilla extract

Made this dessert a couple years back after being really curious as to why Saiki k loved it so much and I became enlightened after trying it. I love new ways to consume coffee and this is definetly one of the more unique ones and tasty ones! The cold helly texture is lovely and when you eat it with the cream mixture its soo rich and it helps balance out the bitterness from the coffee. You can top with coffe beans, chocolate shavings, cherries, condensed milk and more. Will definitely be making again soon!
